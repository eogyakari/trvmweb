Your Window to the Field

The Mission Newsletter is our weekly communique from the front lines. Each edition carries fresh updates from our missionaries in Ghana, Liberia, Kenya, and Indonesia — real stories, real people, real encounters with God.

Whether it is a report from a crusade that saw hundreds respond to the Gospel, a testimony of healing, a prayer request from a missionary in a challenging field, or an opportunity to give to an urgent need — the newsletter keeps you informed, connected, and engaged with the mission.

"How then will they call on him in whom they have not believed? And how are they to believe in him of whom they have never heard?"

— Romans 10:14

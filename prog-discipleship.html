Why Discipleship Matters

The Great Commission is not merely to evangelise — it is to make disciples. Jesus spent three years not just preaching to crowds, but deliberately investing in twelve men who would turn the world upside down. At TRVM, we believe that lasting transformation requires deep discipleship, not just a single conversion experience.

Our discipleship programmes are built on the conviction that every believer is called to grow, every leader is called to multiply, and every church is called to make disciples who make disciples.

"And what you have heard from me in the presence of many witnesses, entrust to faithful men, who will be able to teach others also."

— 2 Timothy 2:2

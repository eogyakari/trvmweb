Who We Are

A Vine Planted by God,
Bearing Fruit for All

The Righteous Vine Missions is a Christ-centered missionary organization committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and demonstrating God's love through impactful acts of service. Established in November 2019, our ministry has grown over the past seven years into a vibrant and mission-driven movement reaching lives across nations.

Rooted in the teachings of Scripture and guided by a passion for soul-winning, we are dedicated to proclaiming the message of salvation, hope, and transformation to communities around the world. Our mission extends beyond preaching — we actively engage in philanthropy, providing support to the underprivileged, empowering communities, and responding to both spiritual and physical needs.

Today, The Righteous Vine Missions operates across four nations:

🇬🇭 Ghana 🇱🇷 Liberia 🇰🇪 Kenya 🇮🇩 Indonesia

In these nations we carry out evangelistic outreaches, discipleship programs, community development initiatives, and humanitarian aid projects. Through these efforts, we strive to be a vessel of God's compassion, restoring dignity and bringing lasting change to individuals and communities.

We believe that true ministry reflects both the Word and the love of Christ in action. As we continue to grow, our vision remains steadfast: to reach the unreached, uplift the broken, and raise a generation firmly rooted in Christ — the True Vine.

"I am the vine; you are the branches. Whoever abides in me and I in him, he it is that bears much fruit." — John 15:5
